Sugar Land

For 2-4 Players AGE 8+

Welcome to Sugar Land... a world of sweet adventure! Come and visit some very special friends. Travel the path and stop along the way to explore the Peppermint Forest, the Gumdrop Mountains, and the Chocolate Swamp. As you go, don't forget to visit the fun-loving Gramma Nutt and beautiful Princess Frostine. But watch out for the ever menacing Lord Licorice.

OBJECT

Be the first player to reach the Candy Castle by landing on the multi-colored rainbow space at the end of the path.

CONTENTS

  • 1 Colorful Gameboard
  • 4 Gingerbread Character Pawns
  • 64 Cards

SETUP

  1. Open up the gameboard and place it on a flat surface.
  2. Shuffle the cards and place them facedown in a pile within easy reach of all players.
  3. Each player picks a Gingerbread Character pawn and places it on the START space on the gameboard.

GAMEPLAY

The youngest player goes first. Play then passes to the left.

On your turn, draw one card from the deck and then move your Gingerbread Character pawn as directed below. Your turn is over. Place the card you used in the discard pile.

CARDS

When you draw a card with one color block, move your pawn forward torward the Candy Castle to the first matching color space on the path.

When you draw a card with two color blocks, move your pawn backward away from the Candy Castle to the second matching color space on the path.

If you draw a two color block card and there are NOT two spaces of that color behind you on path, then move pawn back to start.

When you draw a picture card, move your pawn forward or backward to the PINK picture space on the board that matches the PINK picture square on your card.

You must draw a one color block card or a pink card to start your turn. If you draw a two color block card when at the start position, then you must discard the card and wait for your next turn to draw again.

MOVEMENT

  1. Always move in the direction of the sign posts, unless a Picture card directs you to move backwards on the path.
  2. Two or more Gingerbread Character pawns may be on the same space at the same time.
  3. Shortcuts: There are 2 shortcuts on the path- the Rainbow River and the Gumdrop Pass.If your pawn lands, by exact count, on the blue space below the Rainbow River or the green space below the Gumdrop Pass, you can move your pawn immediately by taking the shortcut to the space the arrow points torwards.
  4. Licorice Spaces: There are 2 licorice spaces on the path marked in an X. These spaces were put here by the sneaky Lord Licorice to try and slow you down. If you land on one of these spaces by exact count, your Gingerbread Character pawn is stuck there for one turn.
  5. Play as above until someone reaches the rainbow space near the Candy Castle.

HOW TO WIN THE GAME

If you are the first person to reach the multi-colored rainbow space, you've reached the Candy Castle and won the game.
